About the Band

Formed in 1975 by bassist/vocalist Lemmy Kilmester, the bands career spans 23 studio albums, 10 live albums, 12 compilations, and 5 EPs over 40 years. Most often performing as a power trio, their most well known lineup consisted of Lemmy, Eddie, and Phil. Lemmy was the only consistent member and the primary songwriter until his death in December of 2015.They are remembered as one of the loudest and most raucous bands in rock and roll history.
